How we build your scope

Every project gets a simple, readable estimate broken into Priority 1, Priority 2, and Nice-to-Have items. That way you can phase work around real life.

Priority 1 – Protect

Anything that affects water, safety, or structural integrity.

  • Active leaks & water intrusions
  • Crawl space moisture issues
  • Hazardous trip, fall, or code concerns

Priority 2 – Preserve

Items that prevent future damage and improve longevity.

  • Weatherproofing & sealing
  • Preventative repairs & reinforcements
  • Pool equipment maintenance

Priority 3 – Polish

All the “looks amazing” finishing touches—your curb appeal and guest wow.

  • Cosmetic paint & touch-ups
  • Detailed cleaning & pressure wash glow-ups
  • Small upgrades that visually elevate the space
